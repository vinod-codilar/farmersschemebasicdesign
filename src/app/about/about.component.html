Know-Your-Customer

Know-Your-Customer (KYC) is the process we use to verify the identity and origin of finance of users in our digital market place.
Thus, preventing any money laundering and the financing of terrorist activities over our platform, whilst creating a network with only trustworthy agricultural buyers and sellers.

KYC for sellers

Seller accounts are verified by Agri MP and our partners Lemonway & BNP Paribas.
They are responsible for reviewing the company's registration document, proof of I.D. and user residence, and proof of company IBAN issues by that seller's bank.

KYC for buyers

Buyer accounts are verified by Agri MP and our partners Lemonway & BNP Paribas.
The buyer KYC process is equal to the seller’s, but is not mandatory unless the buyer want to retract funds from his Agri MP wallet.

Certified B Corporation

The B Corporations are a new kind of business that balances purpose and profit.
They are legally required to consider the impact of their decisions on their workers, customers, suppliers, community, and the environment.
This is a community of leaders, driving a global movement of people using business as a force for good.
